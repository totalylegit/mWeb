Art Media Types

Drawing is a versatile art form that can be executed using various media types, each offering unique textures, effects, and techniques. Some common drawing media include:

  • Pencil: Graphite pencils are widely used for sketching and detailed drawings. They come in different hardness levels, allowing artists to create a range of tones from light to dark.
  • Digital Drawing: Utilizing software and digital tools, artists can create drawings on tablets or computers. This medium offers flexibility with layers, undo options, and a vast array of brushes and effects.
  • 3D 3D software including Houdini and Blender are used to make 3D art. Although it is much harder than digital 2D art and Pencil art. The extra axis requires double the work of traditionally 2D art. All that time to be projected onto a 2D surface.
