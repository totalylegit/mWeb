My Musical Journey

I have a passion for music that has been a significant part of my life. I have passed Grade 10 Piano with the Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) and actively play the violin in my school's string orchestra. Music has always been a source of joy and creativity for me, and I enjoy exploring different genres and styles.

In addition to performing, I also dabble in music production using FL Studio. I love creating my own compositions and experimenting with various sounds and effects. Music production allows me to express myself in new ways and brings a unique dimension to my musical experience.

Skills & Tools

  • Piano (Grade 10 RCM)
  • Violin (School Orchestra)
  • Music Theory
  • FL Studio (Music Production)
