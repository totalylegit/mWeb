My Career Path steps

I want to become a doctor (Cardiac Surgeon) - What is needed?
Become a Fellow of the Royal college of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada (RCPSC) - How to become member?
Certification Exam from RCPSC for cardiac surgery - What's before that?
Obtain medical license from Medical council of Canada (MCC) - How to obtain license?
Pass Medical Council of Canada Qualifying Examination (MCCQE) - What is before that?
Complete medical residency (Direct-entry 6 year program) - How to get into residency?
Graduate from medical school (MD degree) - How to get into medical school?
Complete undergraduate degree (BSc), Pass MCAT, High Score in CASPER, High Score in CARS - How to complete these steps?

  • Study for MCAT

  • Quick and logical decision making for CARS
  • Maintain high GPA in undergraduate degree
